## Project Overview

The Guangzhou Medical Beauty Trading Platform (ANGELAPEACH) is a WeChat ecosystem trading platform providing traceability and warranty services for medical beauty products, including user H5 end and institution management backend. The project needs to run stably within the WeChat environment, ensuring normal operation of WeChat official account related business.

![preview](/articles/launch-your-porfolio.jpg)

## Tech Stack

- **Vue.js** - Progressive JavaScript Framework
- **Naive UI** - Vue 3 Component Library
- **Vite** - Next Generation Frontend Build Tool
- **Tailwind CSS** - Atomic CSS Framework

## My Responsibilities

### 1. H5 Warranty Card Binding Page

Independently completed the full-process development of the H5 warranty card binding page embedded in the WeChat official account, including user registration, product binding, and information query functions.

### 2. Institution Management Backend

Independently designed and developed the supporting institution management backend, supporting management and tracking of warranty information and user data.

### 3. WeChat Environment Adaptation

Ensured all functions run stably within the WeChat environment, guaranteeing normal operation of the "ANGELAPEACH" WeChat official account related business.

## Project Highlights

- ✅ Complete warranty card binding process
- ✅ User registration and information query functionality
- ✅ Institution management backend supporting data management and tracking
- ✅ Perfect WeChat environment adaptation

## Project Results

Successfully delivered a stable and reliable medical beauty product warranty platform, providing users with convenient warranty services while offering efficient management tools for institutions, ensuring normal operation of WeChat official account business.
